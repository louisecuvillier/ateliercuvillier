About the artist

A quiet response
to a loud world.

I create paintings as a quiet response to the world I move through. Alongside my work in human rights, I spend much of my time engaging with complexity, urgency and difficult realities. Painting becomes a necessary shift — towards softness, light and something more enduring.

My work is rooted in landscapes shaped by water, air and Scandinavian light. These elements feel constant, even as everything else changes. I am drawn to how light moves across a surface, how water holds and reflects, and how both can create a sense of stillness while often shifting form.

Many of the places I paint are imagined or remembered — spaces to step into. As a child I would look at paintings and feel transported. This work continues that instinct: to offer a quiet place to arrive, without needing to go anywhere.

Each piece is created to bring a sense of calm, presence and subtle beauty into everyday life.

How it works

From studio to your wall

01

Choose or commission

Browse available works and prints, or reach out to commission a piece created especially for you. I work with your brief, your space, and your sense of what you need on the wall.

02

Printed or painted

Original works are one-of-a-kind oil or mixed media paintings. Fine art prints are produced in limited editions on archival paper that will last generations. Both are signed and certificated.

03

Carefully packed & shipped

All works are carefully packed and shipped worldwide. Originals arrive with documentation and a personal note. Prints are rolled in archival tubes or flat-packed depending on size.

"Beauty is not a luxury. For those of us who work daily with what is difficult, it is a form of survival — and of hope."
