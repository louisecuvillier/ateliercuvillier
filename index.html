The Studio

Made Slowly, by Hand

Atelier Cuvillier is a small studio in Gothenburg, Sweden — where paintings, clay, and wool become quiet objects for living with.

I paint because I need to. After years of working in human rights — in conflict zones, in difficult rooms, with difficult realities — making something with my hands became necessary. A counterweight. A way back to what is enduring.

"I want to make pieces so quietly beautiful that when you come upon them, the noise of the day subsides."

That is what I hope these pieces offer you too: a small place of arrival. Something that makes your home feel more like itself.

How It's Made

From Studio to Home

I

Made by Hand

Every piece is painted, formed, or felted individually in the Gothenburg studio — no mass production, no shortcuts.

II

Made in Small Runs

Paintings are one of a kind. Clay and wool pieces are made in limited quantities — when they're gone, they're gone.

III

Shipped with Care

Each piece is carefully packaged and shipped directly from the studio in Gothenburg, Sweden.

Shipping & Ordering

How to Bring a Piece Home

All pieces ship from the studio in Gothenburg, Sweden.

  • Sweden: 2–4 business days, tracked shipping
  • EU & Nordics: 5–9 business days, tracked & insured
  • Rest of world: 7–14 business days, tracked & insured
  • Original paintings are carefully packed in protective board and shipped rolled or boxed depending on size
  • Customs duties for non-EU orders are the buyer's responsibility

To purchase, send an inquiry using the form — I'll confirm availability, exact shipping cost to your location, and send a secure payment link directly.

You can also browse and purchase directly through the Etsy shop.

Atelier Cuvillier is a Gothenburg-based art studio creating original Scandinavian paintings, handformed clay objects, and hand-felted wool home decor. Each piece is made by hand in small quantities, drawing on the Nordic landscape of light, water, and stillness. The studio is run by Louise Cuvillier, an artist based in Sweden. Shop original art and handmade home objects directly through our Etsy shop, or read more about the studio's story and process. For consulting and human rights advisory work by the same founder, visit The Dignity Practice.
